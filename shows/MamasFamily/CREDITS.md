## Video content

**The Internet Archive** — https://archive.org

[Mama's Family (complete TV series)](https://archive.org/details/mamas-family-Lando18)
— all 130 episodes across 6 seasons (NBC/syndication, 1983-90), about 21
minutes each, as MP4. Support the Archive: https://archive.org/donate

*Mama's Family* is the property of its respective rights holders; this project
streams a publicly hosted recording and hosts no video content itself.

## Source notes

A clean, complete upload: `S01E01 (Title).mp4` throughout, no gaps, season
sizes matching the broadcast record exactly (13, 22, 25, 25, 25, 20). Titles in
the guide come from the record — parsed from the raw wikitext of Wikipedia's
episode list — rather than from the filenames, per `ADDING_A_SHOW.md` §1c.
