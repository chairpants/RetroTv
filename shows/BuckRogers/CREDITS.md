## Video content

**The Internet Archive** — https://archive.org

[Buck Rogers in the 25th Century - Complete Series 1979](https://archive.org/details/buck-rogers-in-the-25th-century-complete-series-1979)
— the full run across 2 seasons (NBC, 1979-81). Support the Archive:
https://archive.org/donate

*Buck Rogers in the 25th Century* is the property of its respective rights
holders; this project streams a publicly hosted recording and hosts no video
content itself.

Every episode is an `.mkv`, which the player handles by falling back to the
`.mp4` derivative archive.org generated alongside it — all of them have one,
checked file by file, so nothing here depends on a container browsers won't
open.

## Source notes

33 files covering the series' 37 episodes. Four two-part stories are single
feature-length files here, listed under joined codes (`S01E01-E02` "Awakening",
`S01E03-E04` "Planet of the Slave Girls", and two more in Season 2), which is
why one entry runs 97 minutes against the usual 48.

Titles come from Wikipedia's episode list where the numbering lines up; the six
that don't — the joined two-parters and the pair either side of the Season 1
finale — keep the upload's own titles.
