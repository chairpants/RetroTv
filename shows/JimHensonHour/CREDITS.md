## Source notes

15 entries, and only 12 of them are broadcast episodes. The two numbered `00`
are the *Inner Tube* test pilot and the original pitch reel — the pitch reel
runs 10 minutes against the usual 48 — and episode 5 arrives in two parts,
listed as `S01E05a` and `S01E05b`.

Titles are the upload's own, which name the segments inside each hour
(`Outer Space: The Heartless Giant`) — more useful here than an episode list's
bare numbering.
