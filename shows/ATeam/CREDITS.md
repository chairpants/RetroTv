## Source notes

95 files covering the series' 98 episodes: the feature-length premieres are
single files, listed under joined codes (`S01E01-E02` "Mexican Slayride",
`S04E01-E02` "Judgement Day") and running about 95 minutes rather than 48.

Titles come from Wikipedia's per-season articles, parsed from raw wikitext.
Six entries have no clean counterpart there — the two joined premieres plus
S02E05, S02E06, S03E02 and S03E03, where the upload and the record disagree on
how the season is numbered — and those keep the upload's own titles.
