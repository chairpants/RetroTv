## Source notes

23 titles in release order: the 13 theatrical features (1989-95), three
alternate Ocean-dub cuts of the first three, the two TV specials (*Bardock* and
*The History of Trunks*), and five OVAs.

The parent item is the franchise upload that was set aside for the TV series —
most of it is HEVC in MKV with no `.mp4` derivative, so Kai and Super were
unplayable and the series were built from other sources instead. These film
folders are the exception: all 23 have derivatives and were re-checked before
listing.

Three films in that item stay out because they are **not** playable, and no
replacement was found: *Dragon Ball Super: Broly*, and by extension the five
Dragon Ball (1986) movies and GT's *A Hero's Legacy*, which sit in other
folders of the same item and are all dead. *Dragon Ball Super: Hero* does play
but belongs to Super rather than Z.

## Format note

`segments: "film"`, like MonsterVision — the episode list shows each film's
name and REW/FF jumps to the start or end of the feature rather than paging
between titles.
