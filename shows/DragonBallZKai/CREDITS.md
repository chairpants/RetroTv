## Source notes

This is the **edited Nicktoons broadcast**, not the uncut Kai. It covers the
first four sagas — Saiyan through Cell Games — in 98 episodes, which is where
the original 2009-11 Kai run ended; the later Final Chapters episodes are a
separate production and aren't here. Episodes are cut for a children's
timeslot, as the upload's own `[Cut]` filenames say.

Complete and gapless at 98, with the count matching the broadcast record for
those four sagas exactly, so the numbering is trusted and titles come from the
record — parsed from the raw wikitext of Wikipedia's episode list. All 98
matched their slot. `seasonNames` gives each saga its real name instead of a
bare number.

The item holds 142 files because 44 episodes appear twice: once in the
per-season folders and again loose at the root. Only the foldered set is
listed.

Every episode is an `.avi` — unusual here — played through the `.mp4`
derivative archive.org generated alongside; all 98 have one.

**Kai was previously absent.** The franchise item everything else was first
built from had only 1 of its 167 Kai episodes playable, so there was nothing to
list until this upload.
