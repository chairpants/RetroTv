## Video content

**The Internet Archive** — https://archive.org

[V - Complete Series 1983](https://archive.org/details/v-complete-series-1983)
— the 1983 miniseries, its 1984 sequel, and all 19 episodes of the weekly
series (NBC, 1983-85). Support the Archive: https://archive.org/donate

*V* is the property of its respective rights holders; this project streams a
publicly hosted recording and hosts no video content itself.

Every episode is an `.mkv`, which the player handles by falling back to the
`.mp4` derivative archive.org generated alongside it — all of them have one,
checked file by file, so nothing here depends on a container browsers won't
open.

## Source notes

Two things are bundled here, and the guide keeps them apart the way the upload
does. Season 0 is the miniseries material — *The Arrival* and the two parts of
*The Final Battle* — where one entry runs over three hours. Season 1 is the
19-episode weekly series that followed.

Titles are the upload's own. Unlike the other shows added alongside it, its
labels are already specific and consistent, and the miniseries/series split
makes a positional match against a single episode list unreliable rather than
safer.
