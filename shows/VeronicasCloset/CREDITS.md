https://archive.org/donate

*Veronica's Closet* is the property of its respective rights holders; this project
streams a publicly hosted recording and hosts no video content itself.

## Source notes

Each episode has both .avi (original Cinepack) and .mp4 (derivative h.264) versions.
The player will use the .mp4 derivatives as they are browser-compatible.

## Captions
