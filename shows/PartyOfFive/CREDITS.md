https://archive.org/donate

streams a publicly hosted recording and hosts no video content itself.

## Source notes

Complete and cleanly named. Titles come from Wikipedia's episode list, parsed
from raw wikitext; two slots (S02E21 and S02E22) have no counterpart there and
keep the upload's own titles.

One file is not an episode — a "Complete Series on DVD" promo — and is not
listed.
