## Source notes

Each season is stored in a separate archive item with different filename conventions:
- Seasons 1-4: "Mister Ed SXXEYY Episode Title.mp4"
- Season 5: "5xYY - \"Episode Title\".mp4"
- Season 6: "6xYY - \"Episode Title\".mp4"

Season 6 was the final season and contains only 13 episodes (the series was
cancelled mid-season). All 143 episodes are present.
