## Video content

**The Internet Archive** — https://archive.org

[Soul Man](https://archive.org/details/soul-man-1997) — Complete series, 25 episodes across 2 seasons (3 in Season 1, 22 in Season 2). Support the Archive:
https://archive.org/donate

*Soul Man* is the property of its respective rights holders; this project
streams a publicly hosted recording and hosts no video content itself.

## Source notes

Episode filenames use periods instead of spaces. Season 1 episodes have simpler
naming; Season 2 episodes include "TVRip.x264.aac" in their filenames except
for episodes 15-22 which use a different naming scheme.

## Captions
