## Video content

**The Internet Archive** — https://archive.org

This player streams
[a single archive.org item](https://archive.org/details/stephen.-kings.-it.-1990.1080-p.-bluray.-x-264-riprg)
bundling the complete ABC miniseries (November 1990) as one ~3-hour file —
both broadcast nights merged into one recording, sourced from a 1080p
Blu-ray rip. (An earlier version of this player used
[a different item](https://archive.org/details/stephen-kings-it-1990_202510)
whose audio turned out to be dubbed rather than the original English track
— swapped out once that surfaced.) Deep gratitude to whoever assembled and
uploaded it. Support the Archive: https://archive.org/donate

*Stephen King's IT* is the property of its respective rights holders; this
project streams a publicly hosted recording and hosts no video content
itself.

  served via [CDNFonts](https://www.cdnfonts.com/vcr-osd-mono.font).
