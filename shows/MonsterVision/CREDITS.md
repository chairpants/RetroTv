## Video content

**The Internet Archive** — https://archive.org

76 broadcasts of *MonsterVision with Joe Bob Briggs* (TNT, 1994-2000) —
movie, host segments, and period commercials, all captured in one
recording, same format as this player's Sci-Fi Saturday Anime sibling.

Almost all of them come from
[one giant archive.org item](https://archive.org/details/joe-bob-briggs-monstervision)
that bundles roughly 100 individual broadcasts as separate files. A dozen
more exist as their own single-item uploads elsewhere on archive.org, but
turned out to be near-exact duplicates (matching runtime to the second) of
what's already in the big item, so those were skipped. Two titles —
*Godzilla vs. Mothra* and *The Devil's Rain* — had a meaningfully longer,
more complete cut in their own standalone item, so that version replaces
the shorter one from the big item. Three titles — *Duel*, *The Fear*, and
*The Horror at 37,000 Feet* — only exist in their own standalone item, not
in the big one at all.

Deep gratitude to whoever recorded, assembled, and uploaded all of this.
Support the Archive: https://archive.org/donate

*MonsterVision* and the films it presented are the property of their
respective rights holders; this project streams publicly hosted
recordings and hosts no video content itself.

## What got left out

Two files inside the big item were too short to be real broadcasts — a
22-minute *Star Trek II* clip and a 21-minute John Waters interview — and
were dropped, along with 3 exact-duplicate re-uploads within the item
itself (the same broadcast saved twice under slightly different
filenames). The big item also holds a huge separate collection of Joe
Bob's later show *The Lost Drive-In* (short riff-only segments with no
movie attached, a different format entirely) — none of that was included
here.

## Format note

No broadcast dates survive in any of the source metadata, so every entry
is listed as undated, same treatment used for the undated entries in
Sci-Fi Saturday Anime.
