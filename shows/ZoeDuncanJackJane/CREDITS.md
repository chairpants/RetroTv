## Source notes

Complete and cleanly named (`1x01 - Pilot.mp4`), splitting 13 and 13 across the
two seasons exactly as the series did.

Titles here are the upload's own rather than from an episode list — they are
already specific and consistent, and this short-lived series has no per-season
article to match against positionally, so relabelling would have added risk
without adding accuracy.

The recording is an off-air capture: a station bug sits in the lower left of
the picture throughout.
