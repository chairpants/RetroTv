## Source notes

Complete at 25, numbered `S1E01`-`S1E25` with no gaps, matching the broadcast
record exactly.

Titles come from the record, parsed from the raw wikitext of Wikipedia's
episode list. Ten of the 25 differ from the upload's own labels, but every one
is the same episode under a different translation of the same Japanese title —
the upload calls episode 24 "Eclipse" where the record says "The Great
Eclipse", 25 "Perpetual Time" against "Time of Eternity", and so on. Nothing is
misplaced; only the translator differs.

Every episode is an `.mkv` played through the `.mp4` derivative archive.org
generated alongside; all 25 have one.
