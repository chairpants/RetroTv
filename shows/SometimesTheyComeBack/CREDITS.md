## Video content

**The Internet Archive** — https://archive.org

This player streams
[a single archive.org item](https://archive.org/details/sometimes-they-come-back-cbs-tuesday-movie-1991)
carrying the complete CBS Tuesday Movie broadcast (May 1991). The item's
original upload is encoded in a codec no browser plays natively
(MPEG-4 Part 2); this player uses archive.org's own auto-generated h.264
derivative of the same file instead (the `.ia.mp4` alongside it), which
plays correctly. Deep gratitude to whoever assembled and uploaded it.
Support the Archive: https://archive.org/donate

*Stephen King's Sometimes They Come Back* is the property of its
respective rights holders; this project streams a publicly hosted
recording and hosts no video content itself.
