## Source notes

Not a season of television but a run of three-minute shorts, listed in the
upload's own numbered order as a single season. Two entries are much longer
than the rest — the item includes compilation pieces alongside the shorts — so
the runtimes range from about 40 seconds to half an hour.

Every short is present as both an `.mkv` and an `.mp4`; the entries point at
the MP4s, so nothing depends on the container fallback.

Titles are the upload's own, which are the shorts' actual names.
